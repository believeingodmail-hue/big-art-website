About the Artist

A visual practice shaped by devotion, discipline, and African contemporary language.

B.I.G Art Collections is built around the belief that art can operate as testimony: quiet, refined, and emotionally expansive. The artist's work blends sacred symbolism, ancestral texture, and cinematic negative space into pieces made for collectors who value meaning as much as beauty.

Every composition is designed to feel intentional and enduring, pairing luxury finishes with spiritual depth for homes, galleries, hospitality spaces, and private collections.

Artist Statement

“I create from a place of reverence. Gold becomes light, darkness becomes stillness, and every figure is held between ancestry and prayer.”

The work is minimalist by intention: enough space for the spirit to breathe, enough texture for memory to remain present, and enough beauty for the viewer to slow down.
