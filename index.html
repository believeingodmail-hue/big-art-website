Artist statement

The work begins in faith, but it matures through silence.

Izoduwa Precious approaches art as a place of spiritual reflection: a space where belief, identity, and ancestral memory can be held without noise. The BelieveinGOD Collection is rooted in faith, but it does not perform faith loudly. It invites the viewer into attention, restraint, and inward encounter.

Edo heritage gives the practice a historical and cultural ground. Contemporary African art gives it a global language. Between these positions, the work considers what is inherited, what is remembered, and what remains sacred when image, silence, and spirit meet.
